About the System

This detection framework uses Transfer Learning on the MobileNetV2 architecture, identifying subtle artifacts in AI-generated faces.

Images are processed by a FastAPI + TensorFlow Python backend — fast, private, and server-side.

Our goal is to promote digital transparency and trust in media.
